# Export Customers

To export customer details in a .csv file:

1. Navigate to **Customers > Customers** in the main menu.
2. To export information on all customers, click **Export** at the top right and select the required option from the drop-down: **Export** (Customers) or **Export Customer Addresses**.
   ![Export drop-down options](user/img/customers/customers/export-customers.png)
3. Once the export of the selected option is complete, you will receive an email to download the .csv file.
